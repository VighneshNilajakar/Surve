Our Mission

At Annapurna, we believe that no one should go hungry. Our mission is to tackle food waste and hunger by connecting surplus food with those in need. Through community partnerships and innovative solutions, we strive to make a positive impact on both the environment and society. Together, we can build a world where everyone has access to nutritious food.
